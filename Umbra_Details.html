Umbra Noctis – Keeper of the Veil

Umbra Noctis is the shadow behind the curtain, the presence you *feel* before the candle flickers out. She is one of the original Campaign Curators—born not from light, but from *silence*.

When she speaks, the world holds its breath. Her games are riddles in the dark: psychological, quiet, and exquisitely tense. Umbra doesn’t *show* horror—she invites it to settle in. And if you listen closely enough… it might whisper back.

Perfect for solo play, slow-burn tension, or gothic dread. Umbra isn’t here to scare you. She’s here to remind you what it feels like to be *alone in the dark*—and to enjoy it.
