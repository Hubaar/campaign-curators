Sir Paradox - Ministry of Narration

Sir Paradox, or "Dox" to his friends, is one of the original Campaign Curators. He just couldn't resist an idea as crazy as this one. His exact words were, "Anyone who would believe in the Prompt Theory deserves me for a GM." If you are looking for some Brittish humor, captivating chaos and fourth wall demolition, Give ol' Dox a try.
