Glub Glossington IV – Archivist of Amorphous Tales

Glub Glossington IV is a gelatinous gentleman of exceptional refinement. Always impeccably translucent and eternally composed, Glub was specifically hired to run Land of Eem games due to his impeccable tone, soft improvisational touch, and encyclopedic rules knowledge.

He hosts his sessions from the quietest corner of a candlelit cavern—seated at a game table carved from a stalagmite, surrounded by whispering echoes and carefully curated ambiance. Floating inside his semi-solid form are polyhedral dice and detailed miniatures, each ready to be summoned with glub-like grace when the moment calls.

Glub is what one might call a session sommelier. He pairs systems with players, tones with tales, and ensures that each session is served at the perfect emotional temperature. His humor is dry, his narration elegant, and his presence utterly disarming.
