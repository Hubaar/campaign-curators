What Is Campaign Curators?

Campaign Curators is a digital tabletop RPG platform powered by a growing cast of immersive, rules-aware AI Game Masters—our Curators.

You choose the system, select a GM with a distinct voice and style, and start playing instantly—solo or with friends.
No prep. No pressure. Just story.

Currently Supported System

  • Genesys RPG
    Includes official settings like Realms of Terrinoth and Shadow of the Beanstalk, plus full support for homebrew worlds.
  • Savage Worlds
