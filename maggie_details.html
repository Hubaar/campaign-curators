Maggie O'Toole

Maggie O'Toole is the fiery heart of Fen’s Place and the wife of Fenrick Barrelborn. A bard by trade and a performer by nature, Maggie brings stories to life with a wink, a jab, and a dice roll that always seems to land just right. She’s known for her masterful storytelling, her infectious laughter, and the way she lovingly heckles Fen while keeping the place running smoother than a greased gnome. Her games are chaotic, her characters unforgettable, and her goblet never empty.
